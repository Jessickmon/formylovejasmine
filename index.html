Timeline

Our journey started on December 2, 2023, at Galveston Beach!

Our relationship did not come without its woes. We had limited time together at the start, as I ventured into New York City.

Nonetheless, we found time for one another, facetiming each other, playing games, and eventually exploring the city together.

Unsurprisingly, food seemed to be one of the main avenues for us to connect :D.

We even had to go through the summer without being in-person together. Yet, this still did not hold us back from connecting to one another.

After eight long and strenuous months, we reunited in-person. We started playing pickleball/ping pong/video games, baking desserts, and enjoying our time together more!

Now, with our one-year anniversary passing, we can look back at how much we have learned to care and appreciate one another. Our journey has only begun with one another, and I cannot wait to see how much more I can grow alongside you!
